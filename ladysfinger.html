TOMATO SEEDS

Every tomato seed contains a tiny tomato plant that is alive but dormant.
This means that it is not growing, it is just waiting. When environmental conditions are right, the seed will germinate.
This is when the tiny plant sprouts from the seed and begins to grow. Tomato seeds were found to contain 8.5% moisture, 25% CP, 20.0% fat, 3.1% ash, 35.1% total dietary fiber, 0.12% Ca, 0.58% P, and 3,204 kcal/kg of TMEn.
